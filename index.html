Entry #01: About Me

"Hey there! I'm Naqiya Bharmal, a Master's student in Computer Science who genuinely geeks out over technology. Whether it's diving into the latest frameworks or debugging code at 2 AM (we've all been there), I'm always up for a tech challenge."

Beyond the terminal, I'm an advocate for debating — give me a topic and I'll argue both sides just for fun. Reading and writing are my creative outlets, and I believe great communication is just as important as great code. My time as a Student Council member taught me the art of leadership, time management, and somehow convincing people that my event ideas are worth the chaos.

I thrive on creativity and problem-solving, always trying to think outside the box. And when the box doesn't work? I just build a new box. (Sometimes it's a hexagon.)
